ALAPPUZHA

Alappuzha, also known as Alleppey, is a coastal city located in the southern Indian state of Kerala. It is situated on the banks of the Vembanad Lake, which is the largest lake in Kerala, and is known for its backwaters, beaches, and canals. The city is famous for its houseboat cruises through the backwaters, which offer a unique and serene way to explore the scenic beauty of Kerala. The backwaters are a network of interconnected canals, lagoons, and lakes, which are home to a variety of flora and fauna, including colorful birds and fish. Alappuzha is also known for its coir industry, which produces handcrafted coir products such as mats, rugs, and baskets. The city is home to several coir factories and showrooms where visitors can see the traditional process of making coir products. The city has a rich history and culture, and is home to several ancient temples, churches, and other landmarks, including the famous St. Mary's Forane Church and the Krishnapuram Palace. Alappuzha is also famous for its annual Nehru Trophy Boat Race, which attracts thousands of visitors from around the world. Overall, Alappuzha is a beautiful and unique destination that offers visitors a glimpse into the rich culture, history, and natural beauty of Kerala.

PLACES TO VISIT

1.Alappuzha Beach

With dense palm groves, a beautiful park nearby, an ancient lighthouse, pristine sceneries, & a charming blend of the sea, canals, lagoons, and freshwater rivers all under one roof, the Alappuzha Beach is among the top places to visit in Alleppey. The picturesque beach provides a perfect location for many of the movies shot here time and again. The beach is a paradise for people seeking relaxation as well as for those willing to indulge in adventure activities like parasailing, beach volleyball, & surfing. Location: Alappuzha Things To Do: Parasailing, beach volleyball, and surfing Famous For: Alappuzha beach festival, Sand art festival, etc.

2.Marari Beach

Located a mere 11 km away from Alleppey in the quaint town of Mararikulam, the Marari Beach is the perfect spot for a relaxing, family trip or a romantic stroll with your lover. The seawater is clean and ideal for swimming. A number of lavish yet budgeted holiday resorts and homestay accommodation options present in the area make this beach one of the best tourist places in Alleppey. Tip: Take a stroll down the beach with your beloved under the setting sun and witness the breathtaking beauty that Marari Beach is known for. Location: Beach Road, Mararikulam 688006, India Things To Do: Relaxing, swimming Famous For: Filthy stretch, palm-fringed bay, blue sea and picturesque ambience
